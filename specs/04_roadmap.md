## Risk Management

### Identified Risks

#### Risk 1: Scope Creep
- **Impact:** High
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Strict phase adherence, regular scope reviews
- **Owner:** Product Manager

#### Risk 2: Performance Degradation
- **Impact:** High
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Continuous performance monitoring, optimization reviews
- **Owner:** Frontend Developer

#### Risk 3: Content Staleness
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Probability:** High
- **Mitigation:** Set content update schedule, automate where possible
- **Owner:** Content Manager

#### Risk 4: Technical Debt
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Mitigation:** Regular code reviews, refactoring sprints
- **Owner:** Development Lead
