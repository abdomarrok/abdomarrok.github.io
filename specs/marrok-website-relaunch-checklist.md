## COPYWRITING GUIDELINES

### Tone
- Professional but conversational (not corporate)
- Confident (you know your stuff)
- Client-focused (their needs first, your capabilities second)
- Transparent (clear on what you offer, what you don't)
- Human (use "we" and "I", tell stories, be real)

### Key Messages to Repeat
"Speed + Quality + Affordability + Partnership" (your 4 pillars)
"Enterprise quality at startup prices"
"For MENA SMBs" (your market)
"Measurable outcomes" (focus on results, not features)

### What NOT to Say
- Don't say "cutting-edge" or "world-class" (clichés)
- Don't oversell (be honest about what you do)
- Don't use jargon without explaining (UX/UI is fine; "synergistic paradigm shifts" is not)
- Don't make promises you can't keep (be realistic on timelines)
