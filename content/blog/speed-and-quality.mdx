## The Iron Triangle is a Lie

For decades, software agencies have sold clients on the "Iron Triangle": *Fast, Good, Cheap—pick two.* 

If you want it fast and good, it will be expensive.
If you want it cheap and fast, it will be terrible.

But here's the secret modern product studios know: **Speed and quality are complementary, not opposing forces.** 

### How We Build Faster AND Better

1. **We don't reinvent the wheel.** We use battle-tested design systems and robust frameworks (like Next.js and Prisma) to skip the boilerplate and focus on business logic.
2. **We cut the middleman.** You work directly with the engineers building your product. No account managers playing telephone.
3. **Agile actually means agile.** We don't spend 3 months writing a 200-page specification document. We build prototypes in days, gather feedback, and iterate.

### Real Business Outcomes

When you launch a product 2 months earlier than your competitor, you capture the market first. That's the real value of speed. 

Quality ensures that once users arrive, they stay. 

At Marrok Agency, we refuse to compromise on either.
